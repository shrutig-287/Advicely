Steps To Book A Meeting Slot

We envision to help our clients develop a better understanding of self along with becoming capable of creating what they want in life. We help you to accept, know and grow in life. Our work is guided by several important values and principles that we feel contribute to the strong, trusting and respectful connections our clients form with our therapists:

  1. Firstly, click the Register for the session button.
  2. Then, login / signup using your credentials, and fill all details accurately.
  3. After authentication, you would have to fill a form regarding some basic personal information.
  4. Next part of the form consists of 2 questions, starting with age groups, example, 10-15 yrs or 25-30 yrs.
  5. The next choice has to be made about the type of counselling needed, from our above mentioned 5 services. Choose the one that most closely resembles your condition.
  6. You would then be matched with the most suitable expert, who can handle your situation and give the right advice.
  7. After this, the date and time for the meeting have to be decided, for which you will be presented with the available slots from the next 5 days.
  8. If you see a slot, that matches your schedule as well, go for it and submit, else choose "See next 5 days".
  9. Observe that our slots occur at a fixed time: 9:00 am - 9:30 am, 10:00 am - 10:30 am, 11:00 am - 11:30 am, 12:00 pm - 12:30 pm, 2:00 pm - 2:30 pm, 3:00 pm - 3:30 pm, and 4:00 pm - 4:30 pm. These slots would be the same for all the 7 weekdays.
  10. After getting the experts and slot fixed, you would immediately receive a mail with the Google Meet link for the respective meeting.
  11. Now all that is left is to join the meeting on the alloted day.
  12. Congratulations ! You have completed all the steps to schedule a meeting and change your life through an insightful discussion.
