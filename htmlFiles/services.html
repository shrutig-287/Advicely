Our Counselling Services

We are here to offer you a lot of services and therapies, which we have broadly categorized into 5 different types. Our Experts have a lot of expereince in dealing with each of the below mentioned categories, for people of all age groups.

Career Counselling

Choosing the right career path is an extremely crucial part of a student's life because it basically determines the direction of their future. Getting proper guidance from trained career counsellors can help us set long term career goals, and also find the pathway to achieve them. This not just includes the students who have to choose a stream for higher studies, but also the ones who have just graduated and not sure whether to continue studies or go for a job.

Mental Health Counselling

Everyone is just so busy in this race of life that they often forget to take care of themselves, and let stress and anxiety overrule them. This anxiety and emptiness or depression if taken for granted for prolonged time can lead to serious consequences, that may be only resolved by medicataion. So wouldn't it be much better to have a platform where users can come and discuss their feelings without being judged, at an earlier stage? Our consellors work with clients to set goals, develop a treatment plan, and gain insight through treatment.

LifeStyle and Work

We know what we are supposed to do: follow a healthy, balanced diet and get regular physical exercise. It sounds simple enough, but for most of us it's easier said than done. We try to practice good habits, but far too many people consistently make poor choices, putting themselves at risk for serious health problems, including heart diseases. With the help of our experts at Advicely, the people would be offered intensive guidance and support to help them to maintain these changes on their own.

Self-Healing and Inner Peace

Finding your peace of mind results in fewer worries, anxieties, stressors, and fears. Inner peace is linked to achieving self-actualization. Self healing from past traumas and toxic behavioural patterns is very important as one moves forward in life. In fact both self healing and inner peace are linked, because both ultimately lead to a stress free mind, and peaceful soul away from worries. Our experts provide support and practical feedback to help the clients effectively address personal life challenges.

Love & Relationships Counselling

The young generation is so obsessed with the idea of meeting their ideal soulmate that they often choose the wrong person, and discover red flags later on. For the ones who are in a relationship already, many still don't know how to make things workout and adjust sometimes for their partners. While relationship counseling is often used to address problems, it can be helpful even for people in healthy, happy relationships as counseling can strengthen their communication and connection.

Although we have listed out all the general categories, but you are advised to select the category most related to your issue, and the experts would definitely help you deal with whatsoever is presented to them.
